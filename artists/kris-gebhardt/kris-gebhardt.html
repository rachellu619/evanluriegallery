Kris
Gebhardt



Kris Gebhardt is an artist renowned for his captivating depictions of the human experience, particularly his large head and figure paintings that showcase the gritty and layered nature of life.
However, his unique ability to portray animals such as horses, bulls, and elephants in a strikingly unconventional manner has also earned him a following of collectors who appreciate his signature style.

Gebhardt's animal genre paintings are not just visually interesting, but also emotionally resonant. His use of unconventional canvases such as cardboard, tattered tarps, and dismantled crates adds a rawness and power to his artwork that is hard to miss. Through his distressed presentation of his subjects, he conveys a sense of struggle and perseverance that celebrates their resilience and tenacity.

What truly sets Gebhardt's artwork apart is his ability to draw on "real-life" experiences, both his own and those of the people around him, to create portraits that capture the essence of the Everyman. His paintings are thought-provoking and demand attention, dominating wall space with their powerful presence.
